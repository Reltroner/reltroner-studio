## 🪐 Aurioneira Stellar System Overview & Lore Concepts

A star system pulsing with ancient energy and sentient ambition. Below is a breakdown of each celestial body, along with extended ideas for lore development, research focus, and narrative utility.

---

## ☀️ Central Star: Aurion

* **Type:** G1V Yellow Main Sequence
* **Diameter:** \~1.12x Sun
* **Surface Temp:** 5,920 K
* **Stellar Lifespan:** \~10.2 billion years
* **Luminosity:** \~1.15x Solar Output

> *“Where sentience orbits light.”*

---

## 🌐 Orbital Layout

#### 1. **Nirael**
- **Orbital Distance:** 0.46 AU  
- **Orbital Period:** 121 days  
- **Notes:** Hot rocky world; tidal locked; experimental energy lab  

#### 2. **Hyneira**
- **Orbital Distance:** 0.77 AU  
- **Orbital Period:** 261 days  
- **Notes:** Oceanic with sulfuric storms; bio-mining stations  

#### 3. **Delphirion**
- **Orbital Distance:** 1.00 AU (belt center)  
- **Orbital Period:** –  
- **Notes:** Frozen asteroid belt; base of orbital Reltronic mining  

#### 4. **Asthortera**
- **Orbital Distance:** 1.52 AU  
- **Orbital Period:** 365 days  
- **Notes:** Habitable Super Earth; capital of sentient civilization  

#### 5. **Celdronyx**
- **Orbital Distance:** 5.10 AU  
- **Orbital Period:** 11.6 years  
- **Notes:** Medium gas giant; magnetic storms; plasma harvesting  

#### 6. **Thrynos**
- **Orbital Distance:** 13.4 AU  
- **Orbital Period:** 47.5 years  
- **Notes:** Super gas giant with ring system; houses wormgate nodes  

#### 7. **Voltraxis**
- **Orbital Distance:** 22.1 AU  
- **Orbital Period:** 83.2 years  
- **Notes:** High-volcano activity; rich in magma-based alloys  

#### 8. **Phalteros**
- **Orbital Distance:** ~34 AU (eccentric)  
- **Orbital Period:** ~170 years  
- **Notes:** Rogue dark planet; odd tilted orbit; source of myths  

---

## 🔁 Special Zones

* **Delphirion Belt (1.0 AU):** Cold asteroid field that forms the **buffer zone** between rocky inner planets and Asthortera’s stable orbit.
* **Reltronic Stargates:** Placed at **Lagrange points between Thrynos and Voltraxis**, serving as **Wormgate jump points** for interstellar transit.

### 🪨 1. **Nirael** — *The Ember Womb*

* **Type:** Rocky Planet
* **Diameter:** 10845 km
* **Mass:** 0.47 × Earth
* **Gravity:** 6.4 m/s²
* **Core Concept:** A burning crucible closest to the sun-like star, Aurioneira.
* **Narrative Function:**

  * Home to forbidden experiments in **exotic particle energy**.
  * Facility ruins suggest ancient civilizations once thrived amid fire.
* **Strategic Use:**

  * Testing grounds for high-risk quantum drives.
  * Source of rare, heat-resistant minerals (e.g., Pyrosilite Crystals).
* **Type:** Rocky Planet
* **Core Concept:** A burning crucible closest to Aurion.
* **Narrative Function:** Forbidden experiments in exotic particle energy. Ancient ruins amid eternal fire.
* **Strategic Use:** Quantum drive testing, rare mineral mining.

### 🌊 2. **Hyneira** — *The Algae Ocean World*

* **Type:** Oceanic Planet
* **Diameter:** 13900 km
* **Mass:** 0.85 × Earth
* **Gravity:** 9.1 m/s²
* **Core Concept:** Acidic, glowing oceans teeming with **primitive biospheres**.
* **Narrative Function:**

  * Considered sacred by bio-mystics as a **womb of pre-sentience**.
  * Underwater temples suggest spiritual rituals linked to algae blooms.
* **Strategic Use:**

  * Harvest site of **bio-energy algae** powering green civilizations.
  * Advanced marine suits required to navigate corrosive seas.
* **Type:** Oceanic Planet
* **Core Concept:** Acidic oceans teeming with biospheres.
* **Narrative Function:** Pre-sentience rituals. Underwater temples.
* **Strategic Use:** Bio-energy algae harvesting.

### 🧊 3. **Delphirion** — *The Ice-Dust Belt*
